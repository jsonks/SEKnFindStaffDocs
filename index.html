What is SEKnFind?

SEKnFind (seek-n-find) is a consortium that unites the collections of 47 libraries in Southeast Kansas — over 700,000 items — for a rich, streamlined sharing experience.

Our mission is to provide the people of southeast Kansas with rapid, direct and open access to an enriched and comprehensive shared collection of library resources.

Member Responsibilities

  • Share most materials, regardless of where a borrower is registered.
  • Fill holds each day the library is open.
  • Maintain patron confidentiality.
  • Commit to making the database as clean as possible.
  • Abide by prescribed policies and procedures.
  • Share information on problems, solutions and best practices
